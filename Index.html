Single Entities:

Entities: Person ID, Gender, Age, Occupation, Sleep Duration, Quality of Sleep, Physical Activity Level, Stress Level, BMI Category, Blood Pressure, Heart Rate, Daily Steps, Sleep Disorder.

Users can select an individual or group from the dataset using the dropdown menu. Visualizations can include individual health profiles, trends over time, and comparative analysis of health metrics for all entities .

This allows users to focus on the details of specific individuals or groups and gain insights into their health patterns.

Relationship Entities:

Entities: Sleep Duration and Stress Level.

Users can explore the relationship between sleep duration and stress levels using the dropdown menu by Selecting Relationship.

Visualizations can include suitable graphs to highlight correlations or patterns between these two specific health parameters.

This allows users to understand how changes in sleep duration may relate to stress levels, providing valuable insights into the interconnectedness of these factors.
